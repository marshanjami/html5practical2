Welcome

Introduction

Welcome to my website dedicated to And the Mountains Echoed, my favorite novel by Khaled Hosseini. This book explores themes of family, love, sacrifice, and the connections that span across generations and continents.

Why Semantic HTML Matters

Semantic HTML is important because it makes this website about And the Mountains Echoed easier to read, navigate, and understand. It ensures that the structure of the site matches the meaning of the content.

  • Clear structure: Sections like history, glossary, and extras are easy to follow.
  • Better discovery: Search engines understand the content, helping others find the site.
  • Accessible design: Screen readers and assistive tools can navigate the site effectively.
  • Reader-friendly: Visitors get a smoother experience because the content flows naturally.
  • Organized extras: Interactive elements like details, dialogs, and progress bars are used with meaning, not just decoration.

About This Website

On this site, you can learn about the book’s background and themes, explore key characters through a glossary, and check out additional materials like reading plans, book insights, and interactive extras. It’s my way of sharing why this story has made such a strong impression on me.

Why This Book?

I chose this book because it shows how one decision can ripple across generations, shaping lives in unexpected ways. Its emotional depth and powerful storytelling make it a timeless read.

Did You Know?

  • And the Mountains Echoed was published in 2013, a full 10 years after Hosseini’s debut novel The Kite Runner.
  • The title comes from a poem by William Blake, connecting literature across cultures and eras.
  • This novel is Hosseini’s most ambitious, spanning multiple generations and countries.
  • Khaled Hosseini is not only a novelist but also a physician and a UNHCR Goodwill Ambassador, using his influence to support refugees worldwide.
